# Supermarket Sales Analysis

## Objective
To perform a comprehensive analysis of supermarket sales to identify high-performing branches, profitable product categories, and temporal sales trends.

## Key Features
- Data Cleaning: Transformed date strings into time-series objects.
- Branch Analysis: Compared total revenue and profit margins across branches.
- Product Insights: Identified top-selling and underperforming categories.
- Trend Detection: Visualized daily sales movements to find peak business periods.

##  Tech Stack
- Pandas: For data manipulation and aggregation.
- Matplotlib/Seaborn: For generating executive-level dashboards.

## Summary of Results
- Highest Sales: Branch Giza recorded the highest revenue (~$110,500).
- Core Category: Food and Beverages drive the highest quantity of sales.
- Opportunity Area: Health and Beauty products show the lowest sales volume and require strategy shifts.
