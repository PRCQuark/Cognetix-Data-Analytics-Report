# Sales Performance Dashboard

##  Project Objective
To develop an interactive sales dashboard that monitors revenue trends, regional performance, and product category distribution to assist in business decision-making.

## 📊 Functional Requirements
- Data Preprocessing: Cleaned the 'train.csv' dataset and handled date-time conversions.
- KPI Tracking: Monitored Total Sales ($2.26M), Average Order Value, and Customer Count.
- Interactivity: Integrated filters for Regions and Product Categories using Plotly/Tableau.
- Visual Analysis: Developed time-series plots to detect seasonal peaks in Q4.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- Python: Pandas, Plotly, Seaborn
- Tool Options: Tableau / Power BI

## 📉 Key Insights
- Western dominance: The West region consistently outperforms the North, South, and Central regions.
- Consumer Segment: Over 50% of the revenue is driven by individual consumers rather than corporate clients.
